## ğŸ› ï¸ How It Works

### Year Progress Tracking
- Automatically calculates current day of the year
- Displays a grid of 365 dots (366 for leap years)
- Completed days are bright, today pulses, remaining days are dimmed

### Goal Countdown
- Set custom goals with start and end dates
- Tracks progress independently from year progress
- Persists in LocalStorage

### Export Feature
- Generates a PNG image of your calendar
- Includes current theme colors and progress
- Perfect for phone wallpapers or sharing
